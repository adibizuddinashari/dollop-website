Behind the Brand

BORN FROM A
REAL OBSESSION.

It's a story about agak-agak.
A story about the generational love for our infamous durian, MSK (our national pride).

My mum loves durian, my grandma is crazy for it (she can even eat durian with rice for lunch/dinner) whilst the kids were only OK with it. My father knows that my mum loves durian and he would always get durian for her during season. When there were too much durian and my mum was silently hoping that her kids would love durian as much as she does, she turned the durian into durian popsicles.

And the kids love it. However, it was a hit or miss thing. When everything is cooked by recipe using the agak-agak measurements, sometimes the popsicle would be just at the right texture with the durian fibre in it. At times, it will just be chewing on icy durian lollies (wondering if mum forgotten to add milk). It was like a blind box, excited to see if we were lucky to get the right texture from the hill of popsicles that we had in the freezer.

Fast forward 30 years later, I perfected the recipe (by attending a gelato course). And mum gave the sign of approval 👍👍. So it's time to share it with the world now.
